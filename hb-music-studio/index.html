I consider myself a creative person, educator, storyteller, and learner. I am a mother and wife, former teacher, composer, and passionate vocalist who rarely sings for an audience anymore. As a proud Minnesotan, I am well versed in the ways of “Uffda” and “Ope.” Now that I have officially, and likely permanently, left the American Education system, I am starting on a new path. I am beginning to see myself as a part of a larger world. Happy to chat with you, I will look forward to any conversation surrounding my compositions or my podcast, Teachers Who Quit.

Teachers Who Quit: A Podcast

I quit teaching.

I am a fully qualified, licensed educator, who is choosing not to teach in a traditional classroom anymore. I’d like to share my experience and the experience of those like me in this podcast. Though I am no good at taking action for any change (far too non-confrontational), I am the right person to tell stories and hopefully bring about a taste for it.
